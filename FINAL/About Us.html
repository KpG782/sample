About Us

Circs Industry started in year 2015 in Quezon City producing high quality computer parts in a reasonable price. It all started from a three-member group called "GAV", which they have the same interest of acquiring computer parts. That is where the inspiration starts of producing computer parts.

The industry sells computer parts such as Graphics cards and Memory Cards that was produced with top notce machines and workers to ensure the best quality that customers expect with its reasonable price, ranging PHP3,850 to PHP46,000. Customers we server already give us a big thumbs up with our services and to our products.

Circs Industry has served over 69000 customers in its lifespan, nationwide!
